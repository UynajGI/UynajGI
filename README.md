## About Me



I am exploring the intersection of **theoretical physics, AI systems, and research productivity**.



My work focuses on turning abstract ideas into usable systems:



- quantum many-body theory and Monte Carlo simulation

- Rabi model, spin-boson model, dissipation-driven phase transitions

- AI-assisted literature review and scientific writing

- ResearchOS-style tools for structured exploration

- solo entrepreneurship workflows powered by AI agents



I care about systems that are not only clever, but **usable, reproducible, and extensible**.
